BowelBuddies aims to be professional network for top quality caterers, nutritionists and busy professionals from all walks of life.

We believe that a number of health issue we face today in society can be attributed to poor nutrition and inadequate physical activities. While we can do little to address the physical issues, we are committed to promoting health eating habits which is essential to good immune system. We aim to achieve this by:

  • Partnering with health professionals such as nutritionists
  • Bring in catering professionals that specialise in delivering home cooked foods
  • Liberate busy professionals from the worries of lengthy cooking after long day schedules.

The platform

The platform will leverage technologies as much as possible for competitive advantage. We aim to make mobile communications first class citizen with our delivery platforms. The platform aims to provide:

  • Service provisioning and delivery between nutritionists and platform members
  • Service provisioning and delivery between catering professionals and platform members
  • Service governance and transactional communications among platform parties.
